Juno Space probe:

Juno is a NASA space probe orbiting the planet Jupiter. It was built by Lockheed Martin and is operated by NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory. The spacecraft was launched from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station on 5 August 2011 UTC, as part of the New Frontiers program

Details:

Launch date: 5 August 2011 , Speed on orbit: 0.17 km/s , Orbit height: 4,300 km

Cost: 110 crores USD (2011) , Manufacturer: Lockheed Martin
